Privacy Policy

Last updated on 28/01/2024.

We, Zidio, are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

1. Information We Collect:

We collect personal information, including education details, email addresses, and contact numbers, when you interact with our software, e-learning platforms, and internship programs.

2. How We Use Your Information:

We use the collected information for providing software services, e-learning content, and internship opportunities. Your data may also be used for communication, analytics, and improving our services.

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at zidiodev@gmail.com.
