Digital Product Delivery Policy

Last Updated: 28/01/2024

Welcome to Zidio. This Digital Product Delivery Policy governs the manner in which Zidio delivers digital software products and services to our clients.

1. General Information

All Digital Products sold through Zidio are subject to this Delivery Policy. By purchasing Digital Products from Zidio, you acknowledge and agree to these terms

2. Delivery of Digital Products

Upon successful purchase and verification of payment, Digital Products are delivered in the following manner:

  • Email Delivery: We will send you an email containing download links or activation instructions for the Digital Product. Please ensure that your email address is correct when placing an order.
  • Direct Download: After purchase, you may be redirected to a webpage where the Digital Product can be downloaded immediately.
  • User Account: For certain products, you may be required to create a user account on our platform. Once logged in, you can access and download your Digital Products.

3. Access to Digital Products

Digital Products are typically available immediately after purchase. In rare cases where technical issues may delay delivery, we will strive to rectify the issue promptly. You are responsible for ensuring your computer or device meets the minimum system requirements mentioned in the Digital Product description.

4. Updates and Support

Some Digital Products may include future updates and customer support for a specified period. Details of these services will be mentioned in the product description. For subscription-based Digital Products, updates and support are available as long as the subscription remains active.

5. Problems with Digital Delivery

If you encounter any issues with accessing or downloading the Digital Product, please contact us immediately at the contact details provided below. We will endeavor to resolve any delivery issues as quickly as possible.

6. Refunds and Cancellations

Please refer to our Cancellation and Refund Policy for information on refunds and cancellations of digital products.
