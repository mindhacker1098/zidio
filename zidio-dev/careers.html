tips for getting selected/
# First thing Choose the Program.

Choose a program that best suits you.

---
**Tip** Carefully Choose the program that must rely on yor current knowledge or experience, so it will be easy for you to work.
---

# Apply to the program

Apply for the position and we will contact you shortly.

---
**Tip**: It's more important to give correct informations without any mistake so it will be easy for us to process your application faster.
---

# Final Selection: A selection certificate with project details will be sent to your registered Mail ID

Once you submit the application, we will have an approximate understanding of your level of expertise, the type of tasks we can assign to you, and more. The aim of the final selection is to get to know you better, see whether you and the team would make a perfect match, and discover your strong points. That's the stage where you get a chance to demonstrate your expertise and desire to advance professionally working on our product.

We are going to discuss working conditions and future tasks and answer your questions about the team, the product, our workflow, etc.

---
**Tip** Just relax and be yourself! We value honesty and open-mindedness.
---

We prepare everything and give you a date to start the work.
